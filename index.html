Privacy Policy for Life Track

In Short: Life Track is designed with privacy in mind. We only use your camera for QR code scanning to sync data between your devices, and we don't store or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Habit Tracker operates as a local-first application. Your habit data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

2. Camera Permission Usage

Why we need camera access:

  • To scan QR codes for syncing your habit data between multiple devices
  • To enable you to share your habits with other devices you own

What we do with camera data:

  • Camera is used only for real-time QR code scanning
  • No photos or camera data are stored on your device or transmitted anywhere
  • Camera access is only active when you're actively using the sync feature
  • You can revoke camera permission at any time through your device settings

3. Data Storage and Syncing

Your habit tracking data:

  • Is stored locally on your device
  • Can be shared between your devices via QR code scanning
  • Is never uploaded to external servers
  • Remains under your complete control

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. The QR code sync feature only transfers data between devices that you personally control.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your devices, you maintain full control over your information. We recommend keeping your devices secure and up-to-date for optimal protection.

6. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete the app to remove all data
  • Revoke camera permissions through device settings
  • Choose which devices to sync with

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since data is stored locally, parents maintain full control over any information on their child's device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date below.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, please contact us through the app store listing.
